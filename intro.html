Beloved_


Every time I try to express what I think about you, this maddening obsession consumes me. Whenever I think of you, my heart overflows like spring waters, rushing with an untamed longing. My cherished delight, my feelings for you are deep and unwavering. I yearn for a rendezvous by the ghats, where time ceases to exist, and we share a lifetime of joy and love.

I never truly understood love before. I never knew how pure, how tragic, how noble it could be. I long for a soul to share the quiet wonders of the world with—to watch the rain, cook food, trek the mountains, count the stars, and bask in the moon’s glow; to discuss poetry and the philosophy of life, to seek common ground while embracing our differences.

Year after year, longing fills my heart. Year after year, willow leaves dance upon the eaves. From the cold winter of our first meeting to this very moment, I never knew you would become my everything. The memory lingers like an eternal echo—your jade-like face, the day we met, frozen in time.

Gazing at your elegance and grace in pics, I can almost feel your warmth, even in your absence. Late at night, I sit alone—restless, uneasy. No force can suppress the pull of my heart; the longing is too fierce, too consuming. Your love is a temptation, luring me into a sea of desire, and I surrender willingly for I got just you to have peace. I miss you terribly, and my arms remain open, always, with a maddening will to wait for you.

Everytime, every moment.. when i see you again
Time stops. The world fades. And all that remains is you.
